MOPAC Support

Users of MOPAC can get support for working with MOPAC by taking part in a ZOOM session.  These sessions are held on Thursdays. 

To request a session, please send an email to openmopac|at|gmail.com.  Give a brief description of the issue you want help with, and a window of time in USA Mountain Time that would suit you.  This window should be in the range 10:00 am to 5:00 pm, Eastern Time.

If a time-slot is available an invitation to a ZOOM session will be sent.

Please have ZOOM installed and working.  In most sessions you will be asked to share your screen.
