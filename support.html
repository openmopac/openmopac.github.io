Support for MOPAC

Most questions can be answered by looking in the large on-line manual.  If, after looking in that, you still have a question, please send an E-mail message to openmopac@gmail.com giving the following information:

 

  • Your name

  • Institution

  • Contract number

  • MOPAC Version

  • Operating system

  • E-mail address for reply

  • What you need support with

 

Normally, a reply will be sent within one to two days.
