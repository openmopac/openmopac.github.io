Improvements in PM6 (Home)

The following faults or limitations in earlier NDDO methods have been corrected:

  • The hydrogen bond and other weak bonds are now represented more accurately.
  • All 30 transition metals, La, and Lu, are now available.
  • Average errors in geometry and DHf  are reduced.
  • Acetylacetone is now more stable as the ENOL form than the DIONE.
  • Anthroquinone is now planar.
  • Although it is not easily quantifiable, the incidence of wildly incorrect results has been reduced.
